1. Birthdays. Get day, month and year of birth for two persons. (No need to make all the prompts, you can just set them like this: var year1=1999, year2=1998, month1="May", month2="May", day1=22, day2=22) If they were born on the same day, the program says: You both were born on the same day exactly! If they were born on the same day but in different years, the program says: you've got the same birth date!
