1. Two-Number Calculator. Put this program into mango/your-name/two-num-calc directory. The program gets by prompt() two numbers and a sign (three prompts in total), and shows the result of the appropriate calculation. The sign should be one of: +, -, *, /, % (should show the whole part and the remainder), ^ (power). Create functions add(), subtract(), multiply(), divide(), divideIntegers(), power(). Use switch. In each case call the appropriate function.
